## 1. CONTENT TONE & BRAND VOICE

### 1.1 Brand Voice Characteristics

**Professional but Approachable**
- Use clear, direct language
- Avoid jargon when possible, explain when necessary
- Be confident without being arrogant
- Show understanding of client pain points
- Demonstrate expertise through examples, not claims

**Example:**
❌ "Leverage synergistic paradigms to maximize ROI vectors"
✅ "We help you find the right talent, faster and more cost-effectively"

### 1.2 Writing Guidelines

**Tone Elements:**
- Authoritative: Demonstrate expertise and experience
- Trustworthy: Be honest about capabilities and limitations
- Innovative: Highlight forward-thinking solutions
- Client-Centric: Focus on client success, not company features
- Global: Respect cultural differences, use inclusive language

**Guidelines:**
1. Use active voice: "We deploy talent" not "Talent is deployed by us"
2. Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
3. Clear headings and subheadings
4. Bullet points for lists
5. Present tense for current offerings
6. Quantifiable results when possible
7. Client-focused benefits over features

### 1.3 Key Messages

**Primary Message:**
"Capable Groups delivers trusted, cost-effective IT solutions through intelligent shoring. Our iShoreTM model enables businesses globally to maximize IT value through access to localized, skilled talent."

**Secondary Messages:**
1. "We understand business needs in USA, EMEA, and Asia"
2. "Our professionals bring specialized skills and local expertise"
3. "Cost-effective solutions without compromising quality"
4. "Partnership approach to your long-term success"

### 8.2 Image Guidelines

**Quality Standards:**
- Minimum resolution: 1200px width for content images
- Format: WebP for modern browsers, fallback to PNG/JPG
- Optimization: Compressed to <200KB without quality loss
- Aspect ratios: 16:9 for hero, 4:3 for cards, 1:1 for icons

**Style Guide:**
- Color palette: Aligned with brand colors
- People: Authentic team photos, diverse representation
- Illustrations: Clean, modern, tech-forward style
- Gradients: Subtle, not overpowering
- Stock photos: Only if absolutely necessary, high quality, on-brand

**Tone:**
- Professional but approachable
- Modern and forward-thinking
- Global and inclusive
- Tech-focused but human

4. Hero background images or video

**To Commission:**

Focus on authentic, professional, diverse representation.
